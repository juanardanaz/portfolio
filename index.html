About me

In the last years, I have been working for agencies and production companies, allowing me the opportunity to have collaborated with a wide variety of clients.

I have a great capacity and constant interest in learning, with great interest in design and technology, in constant search of challenges. I adapt easily to new groups and I feel very comfortable working in a team and always focused on achieving the best results.

In my spare time, I study languages, I do social volunteering and go on long bicycle rides. I also love to swim, read and design interesting projects. I'm always open to new opportunities. Let's get in touch!
